Introducing the Robotics Competition, an exciting event where teams of students will showcase their skills in building and programming robots. This competition is open to students of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced roboticists. Teams will have the opportunity to design, build and program robots to compete against other teams in a series of challenging tasks and obstacles. The competition will be judged by a panel of experts in the field of robotics, who will evaluate the robots based on factors such as functionality, design, and performance. In addition to the competition, there will also be workshops and seminars led by industry professionals . So gather your team, sharpen your skills, and get ready to compete against the best in the field of robotics!

Smart store

Smart store refers to the use of advanced technologies, such as sensors, artificial intelligence, and data analytics, to enhance the customer shopping experience and optimize store operations.

smart-farm

Smart-farm is an advanced farming system that utilizes technology to optimize agricultural production. It involves the use of sensors, drones, and other devices to monitor crops, soil, and livestock. Smart-farm can improve efficiency, reduce costs, and increase yields.

Self-driving cars

Self-driving cars, also known as autonomous vehicles, are vehicles that use advanced sensors and artificial intelligence to navigate and operate without human intervention.

About Our Club
Arts & Crafts Robotics and Innovation Club

Welcome to the Arts & Crafts Robotics and Innovation Club! Our club is dedicated to fostering creativity and innovation through the combination of arts and crafts with robotics and technology. We believe that by combining these two worlds, we can inspire our members to think differently, explore new forms of technology, and express themselves through unique projects.

Our club offers a wide range of activities, including building and programming robots, creating digital art, and experimenting with new forms of technology. We encourage our members to think outside the box, take risks, and push the boundaries of what's possible. We believe that the only limit to what we can achieve is our own imagination. Our leadership team is comprised of dedicated students who are passionate about technology and the arts. They are committed to making the club a fun and engaging experience for all members. We believe that by working together, we can achieve great things. We welcome students of all skill levels and interests to join us in our journey of discovery. Whether you're an experienced coder or just starting to learn, we have something for everyone. We encourage our members to collaborate and learn from one another, as we believe that this is the key to success. So why wait? Join us today and discover your true potential!".
